CATEGORY: Blush

"There’s nothing like a soft hint of blush to add dimension and color to your look"

Selena Gomez on Rare Beauty

Product: Soft Pinch Liquid Blush

Shade: Lucky – hot pink (dewy)

Details: Airy, lightweight liquid formula blends and builds effortlessly for a soft flush of color.

Infused with long-lasting color pigments for all-day wear—a little goes a long way.

Mistake-proof, it layers beautifully over liquid/powder formulas without disturbing makeup.

Price:CA$34

Rating: 4.5 stars

Review: This was definitey a love at first sight buy! I love the shade of pink; it's true to its name fr! It is a dewy finish which i was not aware of pre buying but the dewy finish works as well! The pigment is very rich which i love so it doesn't disappear like most blushes do ( esp. on dark skin tones). A little goes a long way so I think it's worth it for the price cuz it will last awhile. It lost half a star in its rating because I need a matte finish lol.
